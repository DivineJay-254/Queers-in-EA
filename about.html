About Us

Our Mission

queers_in_east_africa is a life-saving initiative dedicated to supporting and protecting LGBTIQ individuals across East Africa. We believe that every person deserves to live with dignity, safety, and authenticity, regardless of their sexual orientation or gender identity.

In a region where LGBTIQ individuals face significant challenges, discrimination, and violence, we provide critical services that can mean the difference between life and death. Our work is rooted in compassion, community, and the unwavering belief that everyone deserves protection and support.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive support services to LGBTIQ individuals in East Africa, including:

  • Emergency safety and protection services
  • Food assistance and nutrition support
  • Mediation and community support programs
  • Emergency shelter for those fleeing violence
  • Clothing and essential personal items
  • Mental health counseling and trauma support
  • Crisis response and advocacy

About the Founder

The founder of queers_in_east_africa has dedicated their life to protecting and supporting the LGBTIQ community across East Africa. With deep personal understanding of the challenges faced by the community, they established this initiative to provide life-saving services and create a network of support and safety.

Driven by a vision of a world where all LGBTIQ individuals can live freely and safely, the founder works tirelessly to ensure that no one in the community has to face crisis alone. Through direct action, community building, and advocacy, they have created a lifeline for countless individuals.

The founder's commitment to this work comes from a place of lived experience and unwavering dedication to justice, equality, and human dignity. Every service we provide, every life we touch, is a testament to their vision of a safer, more inclusive East Africa.
